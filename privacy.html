Privacy Policy for LumiBoost

Last updated: October 2025

LumiBoost is an Android photo enhancement application developed by Haider Alfursani. This Privacy Policy explains how LumiBoost handles photos, permissions, and advertising data.

1. Image Processing and Local Data

All photo processing happens locally on your device. LumiBoost does not upload, transfer, or store your photos on external servers. Your images stay on your phone unless you save the enhanced result to your own gallery.

2. Personal Data Collection

LumiBoost does not collect personal data such as:

  • Your name
  • Your contacts
  • Your precise location
  • Your photos or media content (outside of what you manually choose)

The app only accesses images that you explicitly select in order to enhance them.

3. Permissions Used

LumiBoost may request the following permissions:

  • Read Photos / Media
    Used so you can choose an existing photo from your device to enhance.
  • Write Photos / Media (on older Android versions)
    Used to save the enhanced photo back to your gallery.
  • Internet
    Used only to load and display ads through Google AdMob. Internet is not required for the photo enhancement itself.

4. Advertising (AdMob)

LumiBoost uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show personalized or non-personalized ads, in accordance with Google’s advertising policies.

LumiBoost itself does not directly collect or sell any personal data.

5. Children

LumiBoost is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Security

Because photo processing happens locally, your photos remain under your control. We do not transmit images to remote servers, which reduces exposure risk.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can contact the developer at:

haider.alfursani@gmail.com
