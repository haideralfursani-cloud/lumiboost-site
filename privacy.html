LumiBoost Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

LumiBoost is an Android photo enhancement app developed by Haider Alfursani. This Privacy Policy explains how LumiBoost handles user data, photos, and ads to ensure a safe and transparent experience.

1. Local Image Processing

LumiBoost performs all image processing locally on your device. The app does not upload, share, or store any of your images on external servers. Every enhancement, adjustment, and save action happens offline, ensuring your photos remain private.

2. Data Collection

LumiBoost does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII). The app accesses only the image you manually choose to enhance.

  • No contact data or messages are read.
  • No location or GPS data is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking cookies are used beyond AdMob services.

3. Permissions

The app may request limited permissions strictly for functionality:

  • Read External Storage: To allow selecting an image from your gallery.
  • Write External Storage: (for Android 9 and below) To save the enhanced photo to your device.
  • Internet Access: Required only to display ads using Google AdMob.

4. Advertising and AdMob

LumiBoost integrates Google AdMob for displaying ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google’s Advertising Policy.

This data is used solely for delivering personalized or non-personalized ads. LumiBoost itself does not have access to or store any of this data.

5. Children’s Privacy

LumiBoost is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for immediate deletion.

6. Security

Because all enhancements occur locally, your data never leaves your device. This eliminates the risk of data breaches or cloud leaks from our side.

7. Data Safety Summary

  • Photos: Processed locally only.
  • Network: Used solely for ads (AdMob).
  • User Info: None collected, stored, or transmitted.
