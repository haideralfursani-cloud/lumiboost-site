Privacy Policy for LumiBoost

Last updated: October 2025

LumiBoost, developed by Haider Alfursani, is a photo enhancement app for Android devices. We value your privacy and ensure that all image processing happens locally on your device.

Data Handling

LumiBoost does not collect, transmit, or store personal information. All photo enhancements are done offline on your device.

Advertising

LumiBoost uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personal identifiers (such as advertising IDs) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads according to Google’s privacy policies.

Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – to open photos selected by the user
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – to save enhanced photos
  • INTERNET – used only for displaying ads via AdMob
