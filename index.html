About LumiBoost

LumiBoost is an intelligent Android photo enhancement app designed to instantly improve dark or low-quality images. Using advanced algorithms, it brightens, sharpens, and recovers color to make your photos look naturally vivid.

All image processing happens locally on your device — your photos are never uploaded online. You can adjust enhancement parameters manually and save your results directly to your gallery.

App Screenshots

1. App Main Interface

The main screen of LumiBoost. This is where you start — with simple buttons to upload, enhance, adjust, and save your photos. The interface shows both the original and enhanced versions for comparison.

2. Upload a Low-Light Photo

Tap Upload to select a photo from your device. Choose an image that looks dark, blurry, or underexposed. The app immediately loads it for enhancement preview.

After automatic enhancement, use the Before/After slider to compare both versions of the photo. Slide left to view more of the original, or right to see more of the enhanced image. The transition is instant and visual.

If you want to fine-tune the result, adjust the three main sliders:

Color – Recovers and enriches saturation.
Midpoint – Controls image brightness.
Gain – Improves contrast and clarity.

Once satisfied, tap Download to save your perfectly enhanced photo.

User Guide – Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Upload Your Photo

Tap the Upload button and select a photo from your gallery. The app will display it immediately.

Step 2: Automatic Enhancement

LumiBoost automatically enhances the photo brightness and color balance. You’ll see the result side-by-side with the original image.

Step 3: Compare the Results

Use the interactive slider to compare before and after. Move it left or right to visualize the difference clearly.

Step 4: Manual Adjustments

  • Color: Increases or decreases saturation.
  • Midpoint: Controls brightness of midtones and dark areas.
  • Gain: Enhances contrast and fine details.

Step 5: Save the Result

When you’re happy with the enhancement, tap Download. The photo will be saved in your device’s gallery under the “LumiBoost” folder.

Tip: If the automatic result looks good, you can save it right away without further tuning.
