About LumiBoost

LumiBoost is a smart Android photo enhancer that improves brightness, contrast, and color using advanced processing — directly on your device. No internet is required for photo processing.

Features

  • AI-powered photo enhancement
  • Real-time before/after preview
  • Offline processing (no data upload)
  • Integrated AdMob interstitial ads

Permissions

  • Storage access – for saving and loading images only
  • Internet – used for displaying ads (AdMob)
