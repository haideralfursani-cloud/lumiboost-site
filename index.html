About LumiBoost

LumiBoost is an Android photo enhancement app designed to improve dark, low-quality, or underexposed photos. The app boosts brightness, improves contrast, and recovers color while keeping the image clear and detailed.

All processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any external server.

After adjusting your photo, you can save the enhanced version directly to your gallery.

How to Use LumiBoost – Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Upload a Photo

Tap the Upload button at the top of the app. Select a photo from your phone. The photo will appear in the preview area.

Step 2: Instant Enhancement Preview

LumiBoost automatically enhances the photo. You immediately see an improved version (brighter, cleaner). The app also shows a before/after split so you can understand the difference.

Step 3: Compare Original vs Enhanced

Use the horizontal slider labeled “Move the direction of improvement”: slide it to switch between the original and the enhanced version of the image. This is a live preview – no saving needed yet.

Step 4: Adjust the Enhancement

If you want more control, use the three sliders:

  • Color – Restores and boosts color saturation for a more vivid look.
  • Midpoint – Adjusts brightness levels, especially in dark photos. Moving it to the right brightens the image.
  • Gain – Increases contrast and clarity to make edges and textures more defined.

You can also tap RESET to return all sliders to their default values.

Step 5: Save the Result

When you’re happy with how the image looks, tap Download. The enhanced photo will be saved to your device’s gallery.

Tip: If the automatic enhancement already looks good, you can save immediately. If not, adjust Color / Midpoint / Gain first, then save.

Privacy and Local Processing

LumiBoost processes images locally on your device. No internet connection is required for enhancement. Your photos are never uploaded to any remote server by the app.
